Koesno Sosrodihardjo

Bapak Proklamator Indonesia.

When receiving, Soekarno was given the name Kusno by his parents. But because he was often sick, the eleven-year compilation was changed to Soekarno. The name was taken from the commander's war in the story of Bharata Yudha, Karna. The name "Karna" becomes "Karno" because in Javanese the letter "a" changes to "o" while the prefix "su" means "good".
